## üé® Style Guidelines

### Color Schemes
- **Laboratory Scientific:** Light blue (#4A90E2) and gold (#F5A623)
- **Technical Blueprint:** Navy (#001F3F) and cyan (#7FDBFF)
- **Futuristic:** Teal (#39CCCC) and orange (#FF851B)
- **Monochrome Technical:** Grayscale with blue accents

### Lighting Styles
- **Clean room lighting:** Bright, uniform, slightly cool temperature
- **Dramatic laboratory:** Strong directional light with soft shadows
- **Electron microscope:** False-color thermal mapping
- **Technical drawing:** Flat lighting with line work emphasis
